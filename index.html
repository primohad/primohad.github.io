About

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Institute for Advanced Studies in Basic Sciences (IASBS), specializing in Optics under the mentorship of Prof. Saifollah Rasouli. My research focuses on investigating the geometric properties of combined half-integer Bessel-like beams, with particular interest in their applications, beam orthogonality, and the generation of Arago-Poisson's spot through structured light. My academic endeavors are deeply rooted in a strong foundation in mathematics, enabling me to uncover and understand natural phenomena through rigorous observation and computation. The interdisciplinary nature of optics, integrating both simulations and experimental methodologies, continues to be a source of inspiration in my scholarly pursuits.
